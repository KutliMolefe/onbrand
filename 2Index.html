About Us

We are a boutique Brand Marketing and Communications agency officially founded in 2022 by Violet Moatshe who is a former marketing and communications professional with an entrepreneurial spirit, fueled by her passion for developing and growing sustainable brands. This has resulted in the birth and growth of On Brand Marketing Communications.

We support brands in delivering on their promises, staying true to their essence and investing in sustainable practices and people.

We believe that A promise made + A promise delivered = A credible, valuable & successful brand.
